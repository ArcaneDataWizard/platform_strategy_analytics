# Steam Marketplace Discovery & Success Dynamics

## Executive Question
What marketplace signals are associated with game success on Steam, and what strategic levers could a storefront team use to improve discovery quality and ecosystem health?

## Business Context
Digital marketplaces rely on discovery systems to surface high-quality titles while maintaining long-tail diversity. Small adjustments in ranking, pricing visibility, review surfacing, and release timing can significantly impact both revenue and platform health.

This project investigates structural marketplace patterns using public Steam data.

## Deliverables
- Reproducible analysis workflow
- Strategic visualizations
- Executive briefing with recommendations
